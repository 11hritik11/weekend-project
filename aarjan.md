# Aarjan's Productivity App

## Overview

A modern, AI‑enhanced productivity platform designed to motivate users through gamified performance tracking, real‑time communication, and personalized improvement tips. The app encourages healthy competition and collaboration, making personal productivity both fun and insightful.

---

## Core Features

- **Leaderboard**
  - Global and friend‑specific leaderboards displaying top performers.
- **Individual Scoring System**
  - Scores are calculated from multiple parameters such as:
    - Task completion rate
    - Time spent on focused work
    - Consistency streaks
    - Quality metrics (e.g., peer reviews, AI‑evaluated output)
- **In‑App Communication**
  - Real‑time chat, group channels, and push notifications to coordinate with teammates or friends.
- **AI Tip System**
  - Context‑aware suggestions powered by AI to help users improve their workflow, manage time, and overcome bottlenecks.

---

## Scalability & Future Vision

The architecture is designed to scale into a **Productivity Comparison App**, enabling:

- Comparison of productivity metrics with friends or colleagues.
- Customizable challenge modes (e.g., weekly sprints, monthly goals).
- Expanded analytics dashboards for deeper insights.
