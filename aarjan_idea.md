Aarjan's Idea Document
Financing App Overview
The core concept is a financing application that gamifies personal finance management. Users can track expenses, set budgeting goals, and compete with friends on saving challenges. The app integrates other productivity domains such as studying and workout routines, turning everyday tasks into a game.

Future Scaling – Productivity Platform
Beyond finance, the platform will expand to a full‑featured productivity suite. It will include:

Leaderboards and scoring across multiple activity categories (finance, study, fitness).
AI‑driven tips and insights to improve financial habits and overall productivity.
Collaborative challenges and quests that encourage peer motivation.
Analytics dashboards that compare performance with friends and provide growth recommendations.
By unifying financial tracking with broader productivity features, the app creates a holistic ecosystem where users continuously improve both their monetary health and personal efficiency.
